Jewelry Party Packages

by Oodalalee Designs

How to make YOUR Get-Together a SUCCESS!!

  1. I will meet with the host before the special date to decide on what YOU want to create. It’s deciding time!! Together we walk through the different package options. You get to choose from the shape, type of wire, and beads YOU want to create at your event. We’ll discuss things that work well, go over any questions you might have and FINALIZE any creative aspect needed before the event.
  2. The host and I will discuss the event location and how many guests are attending.
    1. What kind of location: home, business, she-shed, etc.
    2. We’ll decide whether having a Jewelry Display for those interested in ‘extra’ goodies for purchase is an option YOU would like to have.
    3. Lastly, the host decides if they would like an ‘A La Carte’ option for party goers
  3. The big day is finally here!! I will arrive at the event site approximately an hour before Party-time. The host will have their favorite foods, drinks, music ready and I will come prepared with all of the materials, tools, and experience to help ALL GUESTS create a beautiful piece of jewelry and to ensure your get-together is a SUCCESS!

Now, time for the fun part...DECIDING WHAT TO MAKE!
